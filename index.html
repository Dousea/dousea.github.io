About

My name is Orlando Timotius Hutapea. I'd like to be called Dousea in the internet though. I don't really do much besides taking a coffee and programming. But those two are the things that I really love doing. I am proud of what I do.

You are probably wondering why Dousea. Just lack of creativity and a bit of laziness. Do is taken from Orlando, us is taken from Timotius, ea is taken from Hutapea.

In my early days of knowing the internet and computer, I really liked playing games. I mean really. From those days onward, I was asking myself, "what if I create my own games?". That question alone drove my motivation to learn programming. And it didn't stop only at games.

I have been living in Bekasi as a student in Universitas Krisnadwipayana since 2018. I have worked in PT Nota Kejar Cita as an IT developer for a project called kejarcita since 2019.
